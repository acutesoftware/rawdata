Disk Drive Prices (1955-2016)

In general, these are the lowest priced disk drives for which I could find prices at the time. The floppy drives are not the lowest price per capacity. Floppies are included because they set a new new unit price, making disk drives accessible to the masses. Flash memory and solid state drives (SSD) are listed in another table. SSDs are a faster alternative to rotating disk drives, although at a greater cost per megabyte.
